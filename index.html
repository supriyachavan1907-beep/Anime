Spirited Away 千と千尋の神隠し

⭐ 9.3/102001125 minStudio Ghibli
FantasyAdventureComing-of-Age

Synopsis

Ten-year-old Chihiro and her parents stumble upon a seemingly abandoned amusement park. After her parents are transformed into pigs by the witch Yubaba, Chihiro must work in Yubaba's bathhouse for spirits to find a way to free her parents and return to the human world. Along the way, she meets Haku, a mysterious boy with a hidden past, and discovers inner courage she never knew she had.

Key Characters

ChihiroHakuYubabaNo-FaceKamaji

Why Watch?

Winner of the Academy Award for Best Animated Feature, Spirited Away is Hayao Miyazaki's masterpiece — a breathtaking journey through imagination that resonates with audiences of all ages.

Your Name 君の名は。

⭐ 9.1/102016106 minCoMix Wave
RomanceFantasyDrama

Synopsis

Mitsuha, a high school girl in rural Japan, dreams of living in Tokyo. Taki, a Tokyo boy, dreams of being a girl in the countryside. They begin mysteriously swapping bodies, living each other's lives. When they try to meet, they uncover a devastating truth that connects them across time itself.

Key Characters

Taki TachibanaMitsuha MiyamizuTessieSayaka

Why Watch?

Makoto Shinkai's romantic masterpiece became the highest-grossing anime film at the time. Its stunning visuals, emotional depth, and unforgettable soundtrack by RADWIMPS make it a must-see.

Princess Mononoke もののけ姫

⭐ 8.8/101997134 minStudio Ghibli
ActionFantasyEnvironmental

Synopsis

Prince Ashitaka is cursed by a demon-possessed boar god. Seeking a cure, he journeys west where he finds the mining colony of Iron Town at war with the forest gods. He meets San, a fierce warrior raised by wolves, and must navigate the conflict between humanity's progress and nature's survival.

Key Characters

AshitakaSanLady EboshiMoroForest Spirit

Why Watch?

A powerful ecological epic that refuses easy answers. Miyazaki's most mature and ambitious film explores the complex relationship between humans and the natural world.

⭐ 9.0/102013–20234 SeasonsMAPPA / WIT
ActionDark FantasyMilitary

Synopsis

Humanity cowers behind three enormous walls, their only defense against man-eating Titans. When the Colossal Titan breaches Wall Maria, young Eren Yeager watches his mother get devoured. He joins the Survey Corps to fight back — but the deeper he goes, the more terrifying the truth becomes about the Titans, the walls, and the outside world.

Eren YeagerMikasa AckermanArmin ArlertLevi AckermanErwin Smith

Akira アキラ

⭐ 8.5/101988124 min
Sci-FiActionCyberpunk

Synopsis

In the year 2019, 31 years after World War III, Neo-Tokyo is a dystopian metropolis. Biker gang leader Kaneda finds himself in a web of government conspiracy after his friend Tetsuo acquires destructive psychic abilities. To save his friend and the city, Kaneda must confront a power that once destroyed Tokyo.

A landmark of animation that paved the way for anime in the West. Its detailed hand-drawn animation remains unsurpassed even decades later.
